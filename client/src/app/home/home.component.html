A WebGL volume, mesh, pointcloud and high resolution image renderer and gateway to the Monash CAVE2

This is a service provided by MIVP to Monash University staff and affiliates. It provides a way to upload volumetric data, meshes, pointclouds, high-res images and visualise the data and change display settings on a web page, and then allows people to visualise the proccessed data in the CAVE2.

Upload data

You can upload volumetric data, meshes, pointclouds or high-res images from your computer or a shared link and view it in your browser with WebGL, or on location in the CAVE2

Review data

You can check and review volumes, meshes, pointclouds or high-res images on the web. If you want to view the volumes, meshes, pointclouds or images on the CAVE2, please write down the tag and bring it with you
