A WebGL volume and mesh renderer and gateway to the Monash CAVE2

This is a service provided by MIVP to Monash University staff and affiliates. It provides a way to upload volumetric data or meshes, visualise the data and change display settings on a web page, and then allows people to visualise the volume(s) and mesh(es) in CAVE2.

Upload volumes or meshes

You can upload volumetric data or meshes from your computer and view it in your browser with WebGL, or on location in the CAVE2

Review your volumes or meshes

You can check and review volumes and meshes with a tag number. If you want to view the volumes or meshes on the CAVE2, please write down the tag and bring it with you

Change and save display settings

You can save your preferred display settings by clicking the "save" button on the web viewer
