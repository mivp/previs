A WebGL volume, mesh and pointcloud renderer and gateway to the Monash CAVE2

This is a service provided by MIVP to Monash University staff and affiliates. It provides a way to upload volumetric data, meshes or pointclouds, visualise the data and change display settings on a web page, and then allows people to visualise the volumes, meshes and pointclouds in the CAVE2.

Upload data

You can upload volumetric data, meshes or pointclouds from your computer or a shared link and view it in your browser with WebGL, or on location in the CAVE2

Review data

You can check and review volumes, meshes or pointclouds with a tag number. If you want to view the volumes, meshes or pointclouds on the CAVE2, please write down the tag and bring it with you
